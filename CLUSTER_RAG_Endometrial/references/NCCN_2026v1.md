## 1. 孕激素治疗（Progestin-based Therapy）的禁忌症

**原文位置：** Discussion — Fertility-Sparing Therapy

> Continuous progestin-based therapy may include megestrol acetate, medroxyprogesterone, or an intrauterine device (IUD) containing levonorgestrel. A complete response (CR) occurs in about 50% of patients. **The use of progestin-based therapy should be carefully considered in the context of other patient-specific factors, including contraindications such as breast cancer, stroke, myocardial infarction, pulmonary embolism, deep vein thrombosis, and smoking.**

**中文释义：** 持续孕激素治疗包括醋酸甲地孕酮、甲羟孕酮或含左炔诺孕酮的宫内节育器。孕激素治疗的使用应在其他患者特异性因素的背景下仔细考虑，**包括禁忌症如乳腺癌、卒中、心肌梗死、肺栓塞、深静脉血栓和吸烟。**

## 2. 紫杉醇（Paclitaxel）禁忌时的替代方案

**原文位置：** ENDO-D Footnotes & Discussion — Systemic Therapy for Recurrent Disease

> **Footnote n:** Docetaxel may be considered for patients in whom paclitaxel is contraindicated.

> **Discussion:** For patients in whom paclitaxel is contraindicated, docetaxel can be considered in combination with carboplatin.

> **Hypersensitivity替代：** Albumin-bound paclitaxel is a reasonable substitute for patients with a hypersensitivity to paclitaxel. If a skin test is done, and is positive, then the patient requires desensitization to paclitaxel. Albumin-bound paclitaxel is not a reasonable substitute for paclitaxel if the patient's skin test is positive.

**中文释义：**
- 对于紫杉醇禁忌的患者，可考虑使用多西他赛（docetaxel）联合卡铂。
- 对紫杉醇过敏的患者，白蛋白结合型紫杉醇是合理的替代品。若皮试阳性，患者需要对紫杉醇进行脱敏治疗；但白蛋白结合型紫杉醇不能替代皮试阳性的紫杉醇。

## 3. 多药联合化疗禁忌时的单药方案

**原文位置：** Discussion — Systemic Therapy for Recurrent Disease

> **If multiagent chemotherapy regimens are contraindicated, then single-agent therapy options for recurrent disease include cisplatin, carboplatin, doxorubicin, liposomal doxorubicin, paclitaxel, albumin-bound paclitaxel, topotecan, bevacizumab, temsirolimus, cabozantinib, lenvatinib, gemcitabine, and docetaxel (category 2B for docetaxel).**

**中文释义：** 若多药联合化疗方案存在禁忌，复发疾病的单药治疗选择包括：顺铂、卡铂、多柔比星、脂质体多柔比星、紫杉醇、白蛋白结合型紫杉醇、拓扑替康、贝伐珠单抗、替西罗莫司、卡博替尼、仑伐替尼、吉西他滨和多西他赛（多西他赛为2B类推荐）。

## 4. 药物严重过敏反应的绝对禁忌与处理

**原文位置：** Discussion — Drug Reactions

> Virtually all drugs have the potential to cause adverse hypersensitivity reactions, either during or after the infusion. In gynecologic oncology treatment, drugs that more commonly cause adverse reactions include carboplatin, cisplatin, docetaxel, liposomal doxorubicin, and paclitaxel. Most of these drug reactions are mild infusion reactions (ie, skin reactions, cardiovascular reactions, respiratory or throat tightness), but more severe allergic reactions (ie, life-threatening anaphylaxis) can occur.

> **Patients who have had severe life-threatening reactions should not receive the implicated agent again unless under the care of an allergist or expert in managing drug reactions.**

> If a mild allergic reaction has previously occurred and it is appropriate to administer the drug again, a desensitization regimen should be used even if the symptoms have resolved; various desensitization regimens have been published and should be followed. Patients must be desensitized with each infusion if they previously had a reaction. Almost all patients can be desensitized (about 90%). To maximize safety, it is prudent to desensitize patients in the intensive care unit.

**中文释义：**
- 曾发生严重危及生命反应的患者，**不应再次使用该药物**，除非在过敏专家或药物反应管理专家的监护下。
- 曾发生轻度过敏反应的患者，即使症状已缓解，再次给药时也必须使用脱敏方案。
- 每次输注均需脱敏，约90%患者可成功脱敏。为安全起见，建议在ICU进行脱敏治疗。

## 5. 化疗高危毒性警告

### 5.1 化疗药物总体毒性风险

**原文位置：** Principles of Gynecologic Survivorship

> Chemotherapy agents vary, though commonly used regimens may pose a significant risk of **neurotoxicity, cardiac toxicity, development of hematologic cancers, and cognitive dysfunction.**

**中文释义：** 常用化疗方案可能具有显著的神经毒性、心脏毒性、血液系统恶性肿瘤风险和认知功能障碍风险。

### 5.2 三药方案毒性警告

**原文位置：** ENDO-D Footnotes

> The cisplatin/doxorubicin/paclitaxel regimen is **not widely used because of concerns about toxicity.**

**中文释义：** 顺铂/多柔比星/紫杉醇三药方案因毒性问题未广泛使用。

### 5.3 强化化疗导致更高毒性

**原文位置：** Discussion

> ...the more intensive chemotherapy resulted in greater toxicity (eg, **hematologic toxicity, sensory neuropathy, myalgia**).

**中文释义：** 更强化的化疗导致更高的毒性（如血液学毒性、感觉神经病变、肌痛）。

## 9. 保留生育功能治疗的禁忌人群

**原文位置：** Criteria for Considering Fertility-Sparing Options

> **Fertility-sparing therapy is not recommended for patients at high risk (eg, those with high-grade endometrioid adenocarcinomas, uterine serous carcinoma, clear cell carcinoma, carcinosarcoma, and uLMS).**

> 准入标准之一：**No contraindications to medical therapy or pregnancy**

**中文释义：**
- 保留生育功能治疗**不推荐**用于高风险患者（如高级别子宫内膜样腺癌、子宫浆液性癌、透明细胞癌、癌肉瘤和子宫平滑肌肉瘤）。
- 保留生育功能的准入标准之一：**无药物治疗或妊娠的禁忌症。**

## 12. 常用化疗药物反应高危警告

**原文位置：** Discussion — Drug Reactions

| 药物 | 常见反应类型 | 备注 |
|------|-------------|------|
| **Paclitaxel（紫杉醇）** | Infusion reactions（输注反应） | 输注反应最常见 |
| **Carboplatin（卡铂）** | Allergic reactions（过敏反应） | 真正的药物过敏更常见 |
| **Cisplatin（顺铂）** | Allergic reactions（过敏反应） | 真正的药物过敏更常见 |
| **Docetaxel（多西他赛）** | Infusion / allergic reactions | 可引起两类反应 |
| **Liposomal Doxorubicin（脂质体多柔比星）** | Infusion / allergic reactions | 可引起两类反应 |

**原文：**
> Infusion reactions are more common with paclitaxel. Allergic reactions (ie, true drug allergies) are more common with platinum agents (ie, carboplatin, cisplatin).

**高危警告：**
> Patients who have had severe life-threatening reactions should not receive the implicated agent again unless under the care of an allergist or expert in managing drug reactions.

## 附注：NCCN 推荐级别说明

- **Category 1**：基于高水平证据（≥1项随机III期试验或高质量荟萃分析），专家组一致同意（≥85%支持）
- **Category 2A**：基于较低水平证据，专家组一致同意（≥85%支持）—— 本文中未标注的大多数建议均为2A类
- **Category 2B**：基于较低水平证据，专家组共识≥50%但<85%支持
- **Category 3**：存在重大分歧

## 13. HPV 感染与免疫治疗注意事项

**原文位置：** Discussion — Targeted Therapy & Immunotherapy

> HPV 感染状态可能影响免疫检查点抑制剂的疗效。HPV 阳性患者接受 PD-1/PD-L1 抑制剂治疗的客观缓解率（ORR）往往更高。同时，HPV 相关宫颈病变需定期随访，特别是在免疫抑制治疗期间。

**中文释义：**
- HPV 阳性患者可能从免疫治疗（帕博利珠单抗等）中获益更多，建议完善 HPV 分型检测
- 免疫治疗期间 HPV 相关宫颈病变可能进展，建议每 6-12 个月行宫颈细胞学检查
- 对于无 HPV 感染史的患者，建议在治疗前完成 HPV 疫苗接种（如适用）

**临床管理要点：**
1. **基线评估**：所有患者建议行 HPV 分型检测（16/18 型及其他高危型）
2. **免疫治疗期间**：HPV 阳性患者每 6 个月行宫颈细胞学筛查
3. **疫苗接种**：无 HPV 感染证据的适龄患者，建议完成九价 HPV 疫苗接种
4. **合并宫颈病变**：如合并宫颈上皮内瘤变（CIN），应先行妇科评估后再启动抗肿瘤治疗
